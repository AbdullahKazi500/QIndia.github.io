\clearpage\section*{\textcolor{blue}{Year-Round Programs}}

\vspace{-15pt}
\rule{\textwidth}{1.5pt}
Many institutes offer year-round internships for interested students that are open throughout the year. UG/PG students are asked to send in their applications at any point of the year and participate in summer programs/research work for a certain amount of time. _Note that the headings are hyperlinks to the respective webpages_.
\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{http://www.barc.gov.in/student/}{Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC), Mumbai}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: 2 months for practical training, 3-6 months for project work

\textbf{Stipend}: unclear

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Engineering (BE/B.Tech): Only those students who have appeared 5th semester and above
\item M.E./M.Tech: Only those students who have completed 1st semester and above
\item B.Sc: Only final year students
\item M.Sc: Only those students who have completed first semester and above
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.iuac.res.in/m-sc-programme}{Inter-University Accelerator Centre (IUAC), New Delhi}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: three weeks

\textbf{Stipend}: unclear

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Student pursuing their M. Sc. in relevant field
\item Student waiting for the M. Sc. final year/semester result
\item Good academic record
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{http://www.ncra.tifr.res.in/ncra/students/external-students/students-programme}{National Centre for Radio Physics (NCRA), Pune}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: upto a year

\textbf{Stipend}: unclear

\textbf{Eligibility}: Pre-doctoral (usually under-graduate) students

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.rri.res.in/careers/visiting-students}{Raman Research Institute (RRI), Bangalore}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: anywhere between 4-weeks to 6-months

\textbf{Stipend}: A consolidated stipend of Rs.13,000 per month

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Students within 1 or 2 years of the end of their undergraduate science or engineering degrees or masters in science of engineering
\item Students from Bangalore colleges, who are keen to work on part time basis for acquiring research experience at RRI
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://iiti.ac.in/page/online-internship-policy-of-iit-indore-for-the-undergraduate-students}{IIT Indore}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: Minimum one month and maximum one year

\textbf{Stipend}: INR 2,000 per month

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item undergraduate students
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.hri.res.in/academics/physics/phy-vsp/}{Harish-Chandra Research Institute (HRI), Allahabad}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: max 2 months

\textbf{Stipend}: The selected students will be paid a stipend to cover local expenses.

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item students in M.Sc, B.Tech and final year B.Sc programmes are eligible
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{http://jigyasa-oss.ncl.res.in/Outreach/student_training.php}{National Chemical Laboratory (NCL), Pune}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: minimum duration of 2 - 6 months

\textbf{Stipend}: none

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item BTech/BS-MS/MTech/MSc students
\item Final year, last semester students will not be considered for training.
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{http://www.igcar.gov.in/project.html}{Indira Gandhi Centre for Atomic Research, Kalpakkam}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: one semester duration (3 to 8 months)

\textbf{Stipend}: none

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Students in Post Graduate Programme only
\item Students from any Institution are eligible
\item Only Indian Nationals are permitted
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.aries.res.in/opportunities/vsp}{Aryabhatta Research Institute of Observational Sciences (ARIES), Nainital}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: 5-6 months for masters students, 2-3 months for PhD students

\textbf{Stipend}: If they are not KVPY or Inspire fellows, their accommodation charges may be waived-off, or a daily allowance of Rs 250/- may be provided

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Summer project students (Masters and final year Bachelor students) can be considered without any financial commitment
\item Students who come in ARIES from various Colleges/Institutions/Universities may be allowed to continue in hands-on research projects
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.iirs.gov.in/externalprojects/internship}{Indian Institute of Remote Sensing (IIRS), Dehradun}}}}

\textbf{Duration}: The minimum duration of research project at IIRS campus is 3 months and the maximum duration is 1 year.

\textbf{Stipend}: none

\textbf{Eligibility}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item students pursuing B.Tech./ B.E./ B.Sc.(Engg.)/ M.Sc./ M.Sc. (Tech.)/ M.E./ M.Tech/ MCA
\item The applications from PG/UG students for summer training/internship of 6 to 8 weeks period will also be entertained as a special case only for the candidate having blood relation with ISRO/DOS employee or candidate(s) from reputed institutes.
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.icts.res.in/program/ff2022}{Future Flavours: Prospects for Beauty, Charm and Tau Physics}}}}

\textbf{Institute}: ICTS\hspace{\fill}\textbf{Deadline}: April 18, 2022\hspace{\fill}

\textbf{Topic}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Pedagogical Introduction to Experimental
\item Phenomenological Flavour Physics
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Target Audience}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Ph.D students in particle physics and above
\item Final year M.Sc. students may apply if they have done courses and/or project work in particle physics
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.icts.res.in/program/gws2022}{ICTS Summer School on Gravitational-Wave Astronomy}}}}

\textbf{Institute}: ICTS, Bangalore\hspace{\fill}\textbf{Deadline}: April 15, 2022\hspace{\fill}

\textbf{Topic}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Stellar structure
\item evolution, collapse and supernovae
\item Compact binary evolution, rates and population modelling
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Target Audience}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item The school is primarily meant for graduate students and postdocs in GW astrophysics and related fields.
\item A small number of highly motivated senior undergraduates can also be considered
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{http://www.iucaa.in/Stu-Prog-ISSIA.html}{Introductory Summer School in Astronomy and Astrophysics}}}}

\textbf{Institute}: IUCAA, Pune\hspace{\fill}\textbf{Deadline}: April 22, 2022\hspace{\fill}

\textbf{Topic}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Fundamentals of Astronomy and Astrophysics
\item Recent developments in the field
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Target Audience}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Students in the final year B.Sc. (Physics/Applied Mathematics/Astronomy/Electronics)
\item Students in third year and onwards of B.Tech./B.E. (any branch)/Integrated M.Sc. (Physics/Applied Mathematics/Astronomy/Electronics)
\item Students pursuing M.Sc. (Physics/Applied Mathematics/Astronomy/Electronics)
\end{itemize}

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://www.icts.res.in/program/swms2022}{Summer School for Women in Mathematics and Statistics}}}}

\textbf{Institute}: ICTS, Bangalore\hspace{\fill}\textbf{Deadline}: April 29, 2022\hspace{\fill}

\textbf{Topic}: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Advanced Calculus
\item Linear Algebra
\item Probability
\item Statistics
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Target Audience}: Women students studying in first year B.A/B.Sc./B.E./B.Tech. or equivalent degree and having Mathematics as one of the major subjects/courses, during the academic year 2021-2022

\section*{\color{purple}{\textbf{\href{https://camp.ncbs.res.in/}{Computational Approaches to Memory and Plasticity}}}}

\textbf{Institute}: NCBS, Bangalore\hspace{\fill}\textbf{Deadline}: April 25, 2022\hspace{\fill}

\textbf{Topic}: theoretical and computational modeling, pertaining to memory and plasticity in the brain, spanning different scales of space, time and complexity

\textbf{Target Audience}: PhD students, Postdocs, and exceptional undergraduates worldwide from all backgrounds
